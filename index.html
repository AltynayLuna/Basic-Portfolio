About Me

Hello and welcome to my first webpage! My name is Altynay, it means golden moon in my native language Kyrgyz. The pronciation of my name has caused a lot of grief for my friends and family in the US. Therefore, I go by a nickname Luna which means moon in Russian and a number of other languages. I am a Russian-English translator with a Master's degree in Translations Studies from Kent State. I am also a certified project manager. My latest passion is programming languages, in particular Javascript and Python. My dream is to be part of language machine learning projects.
